# Mouthpiece Construction Guide


1. Eat your way to the kinder egg capsule. You can do what you want with the toy.
2. Seperate the top and bottom of the egg with a saw or cutting pliers
3. Carefully drill 4mm holes in both through top of both capsule halves. Ensure they are aligned fitting one upon the other before drilling. The hose should only just be able to fit through,
tightly clamped by the hole to ensure a hermetic seal.
4. Cut a 5cm by 5cm square of copper foil with scissors.
5. Shape the foil into a circle with scissors and cut a roughly 5mm hole through the centre.
6. Stick the foil to the taller capsule half as shown. This is the base of your mouthpiece
7. Strip the top of a jumper wire to expose the wire and solder to the bottom of the copper foil as shown.
8. Cut a 5-10cm length of hose and funnel through the mouthpiece.
9. Funnel hose through the other capsule half (the mouthpiece top) to assemble your finished mouthpiece. Make sure that the top and base are touching with minimal air gap between them.
10. We also attached a molex shell to the other end of the wire to ensure mechanical stability. The sensor is easily triggered by mechanical stresses upon the wire.
11. Trombone more with tromboneless!!
